The AIChE Southern Asia Parliamentary Debate Competition 2025 brings together student chapters to debate engineering and sustainability topics.

Register Now

Event Information

Arranged by: Southern Asia Regional Subcommittee, AIChE ESC

Event website: aichesouthernasia.github.io

Objective

To foster critical thinking, persuasive communication, and teamwork among AIChE student members through structured debates on engineering and sustainability topics.

1. Structure & Participation

  • Eligibility: Active AIChE student chapters in the Southern Asia region.
  • Team Composition: Each participating chapter will nominate two representatives:
    • One pre-final year student
    • One final year student
  • Grouping: Chapters will be randomly assigned to either Team Government or Team Opposition. In case of an uneven number of chapters, the extra chapter will be placed in one team, and scores will be normalized at the end to ensure fairness.
  • Communication: All selected participants will be added to a dedicated WhatsApp group for updates, coordination, and announcements. The debate topic will be disclosed 48 hours before the event through this group.

2. Debate Format

  • Style: Parliamentary format.
  • Speaking Time: Each chapter gets 5 minutes total.
    • Speaker 1 (Junior): 2 minutes
    • Speaker 2 (Senior): 3 minutes
  • Exceeding time will lead to point deductions.

3. Judging Criteria (Total 100 points)

CriteriaDescriptionPoints
Content & LogicStrength, structure, and depth of arguments30
Team CoordinationFlow and balance between the two speakers25
Delivery & ConfidenceClarity, tone, and stage presence25
Relevance & ImpactConnection to engineering or sustainability issues20

4. Scoring & Recognition

  • Evaluation: Judges from the AIChE Executive Committee.
  • Awards:
    • Winning Team (based on total points)
    • Best Speaker (individual performance)
  • Recognition: Winners featured on AIChE Executive Committee social media platforms.
  • Participation Certificates: All participants will receive digital certificates from AIChE Southern Asia, recognizing their contribution.
  • Impact: Participating chapters earn AIChE Activity Points toward annual evaluation.
