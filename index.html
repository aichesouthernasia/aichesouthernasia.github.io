Event Information

Objective: Foster critical thinking, persuasive communication, and teamwork among AIChE student members.

  • Eligibility: Active AIChE student chapters, Southern Asia region.
  • Team: Two representatives per chapter (one pre-final, one final year).
  • Format: Parliamentary style. Speaking time: Speaker 1 — 2 min; Speaker 2 — 3 min.
  • Judging (100 pts): Content & Logic (30), Team Coordination (25), Delivery (25), Relevance (20).
  • Certificates: Digital certificates for all participants.
